## How to Use This Game

**Prime Maze** is a 3D top-down maze game where students navigate levels by collecting prime or composite numbers. Four scaffolded levels build from small primes to larger composite numbers with more branching paths.

### Controls

- **WASD** or **arrow keys** to move
- **Shift** to sprint
- **Touch controls** available on mobile

### What Students Learn

- Distinguish prime numbers from composite numbers
- Apply divisibility reasoning at speed
- Recognise patterns across number ranges
