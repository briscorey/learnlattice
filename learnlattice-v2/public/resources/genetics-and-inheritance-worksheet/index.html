Part 2 â€” Punnett Squares

A Punnett square predicts the possible genotypes of offspring from a cross between two parents.

How to complete a Punnett square:

  1. Write one parent’s alleles across the top (one per column)
  2. Write the other parent’s alleles down the side (one per row)
  3. Fill each box by combining the alleles from that row and column
